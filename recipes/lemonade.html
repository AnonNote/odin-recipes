Lemonade recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 4-6 lemons)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 5 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Roll, cut, juice, and strain lemons to get 1 cup of lemon juice.
  2. Dissolve 1 cup of sugar in 1 cup of water over medium heat, then cool.
  3. Combine lemon juice, simple syrup, and 4 cups of cold water in a pitcher.
  4. Chill for 30 minutes, serve over ice, and garnish with lemon slices and mint.
