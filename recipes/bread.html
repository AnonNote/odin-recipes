Bread recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups (450g) bread flour or all-purpose flour
  • 1 ½ cups (360ml) warm water (around 110°F or 45°C)
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional, helps feed the yeast)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil or melted butter (optional, adds flavor and softness)

Steps

  1. Activate the Yeast
  2. Mix the Dough
  3. Knead the Dough
  4. First Rise
  5. Shape the Dough
  6. Second Rise
  7. Preheat the oven
  8. Bake the bread
  9. Cool the bread
