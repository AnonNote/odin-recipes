Cake recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter (softened)
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 3/4 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat: Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Mix Butter and Sugar: Cream together sugar and butter.
  3. Add Eggs and Vanilla: Beat in eggs and vanilla.
  4. Combine Ingredients: Mix in flour, baking powder, and milk.
  5. Bake: Pour batter into greased pan and bake for 30-40 minutes. Cool before serving.
